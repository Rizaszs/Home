As nightfall falls, hearts start to believe.
Whispers of hope, in the silent air,
A symphony of dreams we all share.

Each breath a verse, a tender sigh,
Painting the night with lullabies.
The moon, a guide, in gentle grace,
Weaving tales in its tranquil embrace.

Listen close, the whispers speak,
Of love and joy, the bold and meek.
In every whisper, a story untold,
In every heart, a longing to behold.

"fortis fortuna adiuvat"

by John wick
